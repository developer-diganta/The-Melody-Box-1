A Special Thanks to ISTE BIT, SINDRI for giving us an oppurtunity to exercise our skills on their SPIDER: The Web Crawler contest.

What's in this?

A place to hone your instrumental skills digitally. A three instrument music box - Drums, Piano and Xylophone, with voice control. Just click the slashed-microphone and speak out the sequence and hear your device play your tune! (NOTE: Speech Recognition is supported only by Chrome and Edge. For best results we suggest Chrome.) Also enjoy 3 themes beside the default Autumn theme - a RGB inspired trio : Scarlet Red, Electric Green and Ocean Blue.
